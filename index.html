Facts About CTFs (Capture The Flag)

What is a CTF?

Capture The Flag (CTF) is a type of cybersecurity competition where participants tackle security challenges to capture "flags." These flags are secret strings hidden in challenges.

Types of CTFs

  • Jeopardy-style: Consists of challenges in categories like cryptography, forensics, and web security. Participants solve tasks to earn points.
  • Attack-Defense: Teams defend their systems while trying to attack others. It's a real-time simulation of cybersecurity warfare.

Why Participate?

CTFs are an excellent way to improve cybersecurity skills, understand security concepts, and gain hands-on experience. They also provide networking opportunities with industry professionals and enthusiasts.

Popular CTF Events

  • customCTF A CTF made entirely by one person.
  • PicoCTF: A beginner-friendly CTF designed for high school students but open to everyone.
  • Google CTF: Hosted by Google, offering both beginner and advanced challenges.
